# 🛍️ Shop Guide

This guide explains how to browse items, add them to your cart, and make purchases in The Drunk Sharks Shop.

---

## 🧭 Browsing the Shop

- The homepage displays **product categories as image cards**
- Click on any category to view items inside it
- Use the **search bar** to find specific items quickly

---

## 🔎 Searching for Items

- Enter keywords into the search bar
- Results will show items from all categories
- This helps you find items faster without browsing manually

---

## ➕ Adding Items to Cart

Each item includes the following options:

- **Quick Add** → Adds item directly to your cart  
- **Buy Now** → Takes you straight to checkout  

---

## ⚙️ Item Options

Some items require selections before purchase, such as:

- Colour
- Size
- Variants

👉 You must select all required options before adding the item.

---

## 🛒 Cart System

- Your cart is shown in the navbar
- A badge displays the number of items inside
- You can remove items individually

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- Make sure all item options are selected correctly
- Double-check your cart before checkout
- Some items may have limited availability

---

## 🚀 Next Step

After adding items, proceed to:

👉 **Checkout page** to complete your order
