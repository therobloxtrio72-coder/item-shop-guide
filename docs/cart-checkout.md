# Cart & Checkout

## Adding Items
- Quick Add button adds instantly to cart
- Buy Now sends you directly to checkout
- Some items require options (size, colour, etc.)

## Checkout Process

### Delivery Options
- Home Delivery (requires full address)
- Self-Collect (choose pickup location)

### Extras
- Next Day Delivery (+20%)

### Payment Methods
- RP Bank (instant in-game payment)
- Cash (admin approval required)

## Fees
- 10% delivery fee applies to all orders
- Admin users get 15% discount
